Bridging Lab Science with
Food Safety Compliance

I'm an MSc Applied Microbiology student passionate about food safety, research, and probiotic science. As a microbiologist, my goal is simple — to make sure the food people eat is safe, well-tested, and regulation-compliant.

Through academic training and two hands-on internships, I've built practical skills across both diagnostic labs and food safety consultancy. At Anderson Diagnostics, I worked on serological assays, while at SAS MICRO I carried out FSSAI compliance audits — two very different environments that taught me a lot about how food safety works in the real world.

I've presented at international conferences and co-authored journal publications, working on innovations like probiotic functional chocolates and smart biodegradable packaging — science with real-world impact.
